## Phase 1 — Foundation (Jul 21 – Sep 21)

Tags: **H** health · **B** bankroll · **S** skills · **P** partnership · **Med** medical. The install ramp leads; one or two new things a week, not all at once.

### W1 · Jul 21–27 — Return & set up
- **H** Start the keystone only: wake anchor (10:00) + first hour. Nothing else yet.
- **P** The conversation with Ellie — the year, what support looks like, the protected windows. Ask her graduation date.
- **B** Set your starting rung from the post-WSOP number; confirm caps + online stakes. Live stays light.
- **S** Baseline every format honestly; set up tools (tracker/DB, range trainer, library); book the first coaching session.
- **Med** Book all of it — GP physical, dentist, colonoscopy, hernia consult.

### W2 · Jul 28–Aug 3 — Keystone holds, first reps
- **H** Get the wake anchor to ~80% before adding anything.
- **S** First coaching session; start Advanced PLO Mastery (fixed block, week-1 module); begin daily range drills.
- **B** Start the structured online grind, low stakes — edge games to accumulate, B-games for reps.
- **Med** Hernia consult; schedule the repair.

### W3–4 · Aug 4–17 — Sleep bookends + the repair
- **H** Add the session cap, wind-down ritual, caffeine cutoff — full sleep system running.
- **Med** **Hernia repair**, early and while Ellie's around to help. Recovery begins — strength/lifting paused per your surgeon; walking + rhythm continue.
- **S** Coaching cadence holds; keep the course moving; range drills daily.
- **B** Online volume continues at whatever's comfortable post-op; ramp as you heal.
- **P** Lean on her through recovery; protect the daily touchpoint.

### W5–6 · Aug 18–31 — Movement, meals, social + heal
- **H** As recovery clears, add the daily movement floor (rebuild gently post-op), lock shared dinners, seed the non-Ellie social anchors.
- **S** Deepen B-game fundamentals; start *light* solver-dev now that the core habits are in.
- **B** Steady online clip; first read on which games and edges actually produce.
- **P** Still summer and free — enjoy it; plan something real before her rotations start.

### W7–8 · Sep 1–14 — Full system + dry run
- **H** Add the post-session reset/tilt practice and the Sunday review. Run 3–4 days *as if she's away* — the Monterey dress rehearsal.
- **S** Course well along; library taking shape; coaching cadence proven.
- **B** Lock the online routine you'll carry into Monterey; set up the WSOP-fund bucket (even barely funded).
- **P** A last good stretch of free time before her Monterey rotation.

### W9 · Sep 15–21 — Consolidate + pre-Monterey prep
- **All** Stress-test what's automatic vs. propped up by being home; fix the wobbles. Prep the solo window: online routine locked, health system portable, study cadence set.

## Phase 2 — Monterey (Sep 22 – Oct 25)

Her first away rotation; you solo in Philly. The first real test of the installed system, and the first volume ramp — a low-stakes rehearsal for the Phase 4 grind. ~5 weeks.

### W10 · Sep 22–28 — She leaves; first solo test
- **P** Her first away rotation, and likely the hardest adjustment — be attentive. Set the support-from-distance rhythm: daily check-in, and handle the home logistics so she can settle into the new place.
- **H** The real test begins — does the system hold without her presence? Hold the wake anchor and the floors; watch for the solo-grind spiral (play → eat badly → skip sleep → tilt). Lean on the social anchors you seeded in Phase 1.
- **B** Begin the first real volume ramp — Philly-area live + the online grind, now that you've got solo time.
- **S** Online reps scale up; coaching cadence holds; the course keeps moving.

### W11–13 · Sep 29 – Oct 19 — The solo ramp (steady mode)
- **B** Peak Phase-2 volume — edge games live + online. Apply the 30/40/20 rules; if a soft higher game runs and you're rolled, the first shot-take is on the table. Keep feeding the WSOP-fund bucket.
- **S** A-games to accumulate, B-games to develop; daily range drills; library growing from coaching and flagged hands. Solver-dev continues, but stays behind the play volume.
- **H** Strength fully resumed post-hernia — rebuild it. Prove the system runs on autopilot solo; this is the dress rehearsal for the long grind.
- **P** Steady distance-support; a visit or care package mid-rotation lands hardest when the new-place loneliness peaks.

### W14 · Oct 20–25 — Wind down + assess
- **H/B/S** Honest read on what held solo and what slipped — *note it*, because Phase 4 is this same test for months. Fix the wobbles now.
- **P** She returns to Philly ~Oct 26 — but to the VA rotation, so busy, not free. Shift from distance-support to the daily touchpoint; don't expect a "back together" reset.

The long one, ~15 weeks: a busy Philly rotation, the holiday gap, another busy Philly rotation. Grind the rotation weeks, protect the holidays. Also the biggest skills-development window before the grind.

### W15–18 · Oct 26 – Nov 25 — VA rotation (busy) · grind + develop
- **B** She's slammed on the VA rotation → grind-available. Steady volume, live + online; keep feeding the WSOP fund.
- **S** Biggest development push begins — drive the PLO course toward done, lean on coaching, grow the library. B-games moving toward competence.
- **H** Maintain the rhythm; don't let her hospital hours drag your wake anchor around.
- **P** Daily touchpoint; be the steady base through a demanding rotation.

### W19–23 · Nov 26 – Jan 3 — ★ Holiday gap · protect
- **P** The big together window — full presence. Plan it ahead so it doesn't dissolve into grind. Real time, rest, something to look forward to.
- **B** Pull volume back — this is a protected window, not an accumulation one.
- **H** Hold the wake anchor through holidays and travel; keep defaults running around the indulgence — maintain, don't white-knuckle.

### W24–28 · Jan 4 – Feb 7 — Philly rotation (busy) · close out development
- **B** Back to grind-available — volume up, fund accumulation continues. Move up a rung if roll and edge-rate support it.
- **S** Close the big development work: course finished, B-games at or near competence — Phase 4 assumes they're ready. Library solid.
- **H** Maintain; tighten anything that drifted over the holidays (mind the "I'll restart in January" trap).

- **S** Sharpen under volume, tight leak review. No new big learning — that was Phase 3.
- **P** Support from a distance (Cleveland); steady daily contact through the away block.

### W34–35 · Mar 12 – Mar 28 — ★ Spring gap · protect
- **P** She's back and free — be present, recharge together. The one real break in the grind.

- **B** Grind continues; fund near target. **Lock the WSOP logistics now:** confirm the released schedule, line up the action sales / backers, set the deployable fund.
